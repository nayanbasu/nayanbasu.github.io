Our IoT Prototypes

Currently, we are engaged in making our in-house proof-of-concept prototypes for smart-homes, smart-healthcare and smart-parking systems. We have built the first prototype for smart home automation which can control the lights, fan, AC and other devices of your house through the internet.

Our prototype is scalable to control any number of devices of your home. It can also be used for complete building automation.

Smart Home

Our smart home prototype is an IoT device which can be deployed in home to allow to fully control the devices of the home remotely. This device lets us to know the status of the devices present in the home.

Presently, we have custom-built the smart home prototype functionality by designing our own ciruit. We have used Raspberry Pi and an open-source cloud platform to test the prototype.
